VietStories blog

Better your Vietnamese with stories for language learners.

Stories is a good way for beginner or intermediate learners to practice their reading or listening Vietnamese language skills through the use of small iteresting texts voiced by a native speakers. All texts have English translations.

The website is inspired by Duolingo.
